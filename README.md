# AI-Based Lead Scoring for Business Growth

## Objective
To predict which sales leads are most likely to convert using Machine Learning, helping businesses prioritize high-value customers and improve sales efficiency.

## Dataset
Lead Scoring Dataset containing 9240 records with 37 features representing real-world marketing and sales funnel behavior.

## Methodology
- Data cleaning and handling missing values  
- Encoding categorical features  
- Train–test split  
- Logistic Regression model  
- Model evaluation  
- Generation of Lead Score (0–1 probability)

## Model Performance
- Accuracy: ~78%  
- Focus on recall to avoid missing potential customers  
- Output: Lead_Score used for prioritization

## Business Impact
- Prioritizes high-conversion leads  
- Saves sales team time and effort  
- Improves conversion rate  
- Enables data-driven decision making
